CONCRETE BABYLON

"Where there once was a flowering meadow now lies a desert with a city of concrete and oilpipes...
Where there once was singing, play and happiness now all expressions of emotions are supressed by a dark force...
Where once there was a loving family and caring friendship now only exists coldness, arguing and fear...
This is a place that once was alive but now needs healing before it is to late...
My name is Guadalupe and this is my story."

Concrete Babylon is a self-funded feature film being made by one person, Peter Hertzberg, at OMOiOMO.
